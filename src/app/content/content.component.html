About Me

I'm Fatih Aydın, a software developer who enjoys turning creative ideas into useful and working applications. I have about two years of experience, and I like solving problems and building software that helps people and businesses.


I started my journey in software development with .NET, and it is still my main focus. I work as a full-stack developer, which means I build both the backend (server side) and the frontend (user side) of applications. I use C#, .NET Core, and Web API for backend development, and JavaScript, jQuery, and HTML for the frontend. I also have experience with desktop apps and RFID solutions.


One of my key strengths is managing a project from the very beginning to the very end. I have successfully taken several projects from the initial idea and coding all the way to setting up the server and deploying the final application.


I am always looking for ways to improve myself. I believe that a good developer never stops learning. That's why I follow the latest trends in technology and enjoy sharing my personal projects on GitHub. This allows me to connect with other developers and contribute to the community. On a personal note, I have completed my mandatory military service, an experience which further strengthened my sense of discipline and responsibility. I am now fully focused on building my career and taking on new, exciting challenges in the world of software development.
